Privacy Policy for Inspriom

Effective Date: December 30, 2024
Last Updated: December 30, 2024

Privacy First: Inspriom stores all your personal data locally on your device. We don't collect, store, or share your tasks, financial information, or personal data on external servers.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide:

  • Tasks: Task descriptions, due dates, priorities, and completion status
  • Financial Data: Income and expense transactions, amounts, and categories
  • Settings: Notification preferences and app configurations
  • API Keys: Google Gemini API keys (encrypted and stored locally)

Information Automatically Collected:

  • Device Information: Device type and operating system version
  • Usage Data: App performance metrics (stored locally only)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide app functionality (tasks, finance tracking, notifications)
  • Send local notifications about tasks and financial alerts
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Provide AI assistance through Google Gemini (when you use chat feature)

3. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage: All your personal data is stored locally on your device using encrypted storage. We do not store your information on external servers.

Third-Party Services:

  • Google Gemini AI: Chat messages are sent to Google's API only when you use the AI chat feature
  • Expo Services: Used for app development and may collect anonymous usage analytics

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information, except:

  • Google Gemini: Only chat messages when you use the AI feature
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize sharing

5. Your Privacy Rights

  • Access: View all data stored in the app
  • Delete: Remove any or all data from the app
  • Control: Enable or disable notifications and features
  • Export: Back up your data (feature coming soon)

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Notifications

  • No personal data is transmitted for notifications
